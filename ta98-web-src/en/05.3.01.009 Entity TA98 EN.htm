Notes
TA98 correction note In the published book (Thieme 1998), this entry had similar, but different, Latin names in different chapters: noduli lymphoidei pharyngeales in chapter 5 and noduli lymphoidei in chapter 13. The two entries for this entity were made identical by removing the adjective pharyngeales from the Latin preferred term.
TA98 correction note A typographical error in the published book (Thieme 1998) was corrected by indenting the Latin and English terms two positions to the right to show that the noduli lymphoidei are a part of the tonsilla pharyngealis.
