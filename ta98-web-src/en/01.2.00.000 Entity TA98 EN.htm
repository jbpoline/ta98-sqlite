Notes
TA98 RAT note The Latin precursor plana, lineae et regiones contained a conjunction, which is not compatible with the rules of Regular Anatomical Terminology. New Latin and English terms have been created.
TA98 footnote Regiones The use of this term differs in practice. It may be restricted to areas of the surface of the body or be three dimensional.
