TA98 footnote Lig. suspensorium glandulae thyroideae The thyroid gland is suspended from the thyroid and cricoid cartilages and from the trachea by thickenings of the pretracheal fascia. Loré J. M. Jr 1983. "Practical Anatomical Considerations in Thyroid Surgery." Arch J Otolaryngol 109: 568-574. The name lig. supensoriwn glandulae thyroideae was formerly limited to the thickenings attaching the lobes of the thyroid gland to the sides of the cricoid cartilage.
