TA98 footnote Corpus amygdaloideum This was listed as a component part of the nuclei basalis in previous terminologies, and functions largely outside activities governed by the nuclei basalis. Consequently, it is listed here in the correct location within the pars basalis telencephali (see A14.1.09.401).
