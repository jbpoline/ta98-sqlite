Notes
TA98 footnote Musculi dorsi proprii The muscles under this heading, being epaxial muscles, are supplied by rami posteriores and may be regarded as the only true back muscles. In this sense the mm. intertransversarii anteriares, mm. posteriores laterales cervicis, and mm. intertransversarii laterales lumborum, being hypaxial muscles and homologues of mm. levatores costarum and being supplied by rami anteriores are not true back muscles.
