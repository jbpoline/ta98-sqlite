Notes
TA98 RAT note The Latin precursor pars intralobaris (intersegmentalis) included a pair of parentheses, which is not compatible with the rules for Regular Anatomical Terminology. The word in parentheses indicated an alternate adjective for the term, so a new official synonym was created, pars intersegmentalis.
