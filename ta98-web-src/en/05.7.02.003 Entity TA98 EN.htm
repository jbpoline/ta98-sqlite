TA98 footnote Ostium ileale The ileal orifice is found at the apex of the papilla ilealis. While the orifice may have lips which meet at the frenula, it is no longer appropriate to describe these structures as constituting an ileocaecal valve, the mechanism for closure of the orifice lying in the terminal ileum. Rosenberg J. C. and DiDio L. J. A. 1969. "In Vivo Appearance and Function of the Termination of the Ileum as Observed Directly Through a Caecostomy." Am J Gastroenterol 52: 411-419.
